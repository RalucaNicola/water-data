Irrigation water demand in India

To improve human well-being the agricultural practices have intensified in India. This has led to a high consumption of water by the agricultural sector. Also, there are few regulations of water resources by the government to reduce agricultural water use or improve efficiency of the system.

Domestic water demand in Mexico

In Mexico the water scarcity problems are most severe in the large cities. Water service systems are managed inefficiently, often mainly affecting the poorest as they live in informal settings without access to the water network.

Industrial water demand in Japan

After the second world war Japan had a rapid economic growth. The industrial water use is relatively high as the Japanese economy relies heavily on their large industrial sector. On the other hand improvements in water use efficiency and water management have resulted in a recent decrease in (industrial) water use.
